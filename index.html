1,000 Highest Grossing Movies

By Donald Harrison, Julia Ma, and Sara Nurollahian

This project visualizes the top 1000 Highest Grossing Movies of all time.

In the linechart, users can interact the monetary success of the distribution companies over time. Unsurprisingly, Walt Disney is the dominating company, earning more than any other company. However, the second most successful company is not the studio who put out Avatar or Titanic, or any of the top 10 highest grossing films. The second most successful company is Warner Brothers.

If you observe: against Warner Brothers, you will find the top 3 Highest Grossing Movies of all time (Avatar, Avengers: End Game, and Titanic) are outliers for their respective companies. Warner Brothers, contrarily, show consistent well distributed, successful movies.

The pursuit of consistently good movies is more beneficial to overal monetary success than trying to make one smash hit.

We can see from the revenue brought in by each genre over the years that Action and Adventure movies started getting more popular than other genres at the turn of the century and remained that way until today. This is reflected in the highest grossing movies since Titanic (1997) are all Action movies.
